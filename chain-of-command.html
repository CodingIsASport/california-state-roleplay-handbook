Chain of Command

The Chain of Command (CoC) establishes authority, responsibility, and escalation procedures within California State Roleplay.

1.0 Ownership Board

  • Director
  • Deputy Director
  • Assistant Director

The highest authority within CSRP. Responsible for final decisions regarding server operations, policies, staffing, and discipline.

2.0 Executive Board

  • Board of Directors
  • Chief of Staff
  • Staff Supervisor
  • Community Management

Oversees staff structure, performance, and community direction. Acts as senior leadership beneath Ownership.

3.0 Senior High Rank

  • Head Management
  • Senior Management
  • Management

Manages daily operations, coordinates departments, and ensures policy compliance across the staff team.

4.0 High Rank

  • Chief of Internal Affairs
  • Deputy Chief of Internal Affairs
  • Internal Affairs
  • Trial Internal Affairs
  • Head Administrator
  • Senior Administrator

Handles staff investigations, appeals, serious violations, and advanced administrative responsibilities.

5.0 Low Rank

  • Administrator
  • Junior Administrator
  • Head Moderator
  • Senior Moderator
  • Moderator
  • Junior Moderator
  • Probationary Moderator

Front-line moderation roles responsible for rule enforcement, player assistance, and report handling.

6.0 Escalation Policy

  • 6.1 Staff must escalate issues to the next immediate rank.
  • 6.2 Skipping ranks without authorization is prohibited.
  • 6.3 Internal Affairs handles staff misconduct cases.
  • 6.4 Ownership-level decisions are final.
