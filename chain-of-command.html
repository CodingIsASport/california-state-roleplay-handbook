Chain of Command

The Chain of Command (CoC) defines authority, responsibility, and escalation procedures within California State Roleplay.

1.0 Director Level

  • Director
  • Deputy Director
  • Assistant Director

Holds final authority over all server operations, policies, staffing decisions, and disciplinary outcomes.

2.0 Ownership Board

  • Board of Directors
  • Chief of Staff
  • Staff Supervisor
  • Community Management

Oversees staff performance, internal structure, and long-term community direction.

3.0 Executive Board

  • Head Management
  • Senior Management
  • Management

Responsible for daily operations, staff coordination, and enforcement consistency.

4.0 Senior High Rank

  • Chief of Internal Affairs
  • Deputy Chief of Internal Affairs
  • Internal Affairs
  • Trial Internal Affairs
  • Head Administrator
  • Senior Administrator

Handles serious staff investigations, appeals, and high-level administrative actions.

5.0 High Rank

  • Administrator
  • Junior Administrator
  • Head Moderator
  • Senior Moderator
  • Moderator
  • Junior Moderator
  • Probationary Moderator

Actively moderates the server, enforces rules, assists players, and handles reports.

6.0 Low Rank

  • Entry-Level Staff Positions

Learning-focused roles under supervision, assisting higher-ranked staff members.

7.0 Escalation Policy

  • 7.1 Issues must be escalated to the next immediate rank.
  • 7.2 Skipping the Chain of Command is prohibited.
  • 7.3 Internal Affairs handles staff misconduct.
  • 7.4 Director-level decisions are final.
