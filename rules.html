Server Rules & Policies

These policies apply to all players and staff at all times while participating in California State Roleplay.

1.0 General Conduct Policy

  • 1.1 All players must act respectfully toward others.
  • 1.2 Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or targeted abuse is prohibited.
  • 1.3 Threats, doxxing, blackmail, or intimidation are strictly prohibited.
  • 1.4 Roblox and Discord Terms of Service must be followed at all times.

2.0 Roleplay Standards Policy

  • 2.1 Fail Roleplay (FRP) is prohibited.
  • 2.2 Random Deathmatch (RDM) is prohibited.
  • 2.3 Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) without RP justification is prohibited.
  • 2.4 Powergaming, including forcing actions on others, is prohibited.
  • 2.5 Metagaming is prohibited.

3.0 Criminal & Law Enforcement Roleplay Policy

  • 3.1 Cop baiting is prohibited.
  • 3.2 Robberies, kidnappings, and hostage scenarios must follow proper escalation.
  • 3.3 Fear Roleplay must be followed at all times.
  • 3.4 The New Life Rule (NLR) applies after character death.

4.0 Vehicle & Driving Policy

  • 4.1 Vehicles must be operated realistically based on type and terrain.
  • 4.2 Reckless or unrealistic driving without RP reason is prohibited.
  • 4.3 Supercars may not be abused to constantly evade law enforcement.

5.0 Weapons & Use of Force Policy

  • 5.1 Firearms must have valid RP justification.
  • 5.2 Mass random shootings or unrealistic combat is prohibited.
  • 5.3 Use of force must be proportional to the situation.

6.0 Communication Policy

  • 6.1 Voice and text communication must remain appropriate.
  • 6.2 Mic spam, soundboards, or disruptive audio is prohibited.
  • 6.3 Impersonating staff or emergency services is prohibited.

7.0 Exploits & Cheating Policy

  • 7.1 Exploiting, hacking, scripting, or cheating is strictly prohibited.
  • 7.2 Abuse of bugs, glitches, or physics is prohibited.
  • 7.3 Mod menus or unauthorized tools will result in severe punishment.

8.0 NSFW & Content Policy

  • 8.1 NSFW or ERP content is strictly prohibited.
  • 8.2 Inappropriate avatars, audios, decals, or outfits are prohibited.

9.0 Staff Interaction Policy

  • 9.1 Staff instructions must be followed immediately.
  • 9.2 Publicly arguing staff decisions is prohibited.
  • 9.3 Appeals must be submitted through the official ticket system.

10.0 Enforcement & Authority Policy

  • 10.1 Staff may issue warnings, kicks, bans, or permanent bans.
  • 10.2 Punishments scale based on severity and history.
  • 10.3 Rule loopholes are punishable.
  • 10.4 Ownership and Director-level decisions are final.
