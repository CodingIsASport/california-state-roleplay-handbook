Server Rules

All players and staff must follow these rules at all times to ensure fair and realistic RP.

1. General Conduct

  • No harassment, hate speech, or toxicity.
  • Respect all players and staff.
  • Follow Roblox and Discord Terms of Service.

2. Roleplay Rules

  • No Fail RP — actions must be realistic.
  • No RDM — Random Deathmatching without reason.
  • No VDM — Vehicle Deathmatching without RP reason.
  • No Powergaming — do not force actions on others.
  • Metagaming is not allowed.

3. Criminal & Police RP

  • No cop baiting.
  • Hostages, robberies, and kidnappings require proper escalation.
  • Follow Fear RP — characters value life realistically.
  • New Life Rule (NLR) must be followed after death.

4. Vehicles & Weapons

  • Use vehicles realistically — no constant supercar abuse.
  • No mass shootings or misuse of firearms.
  • No exploiting physics or glitches for advantage.

5. Staff Interaction

  • Follow staff instructions immediately.
  • Do not argue punishments — use the appeals system.
  • No favoritism — everyone is treated equally.

6. Exploits & Cheating

  • Zero tolerance for exploiting, cheating, or hacking.
  • No mod menus, scripts, or client-side manipulation.

7. NSFW / ERP Policy

  • No sexual content or erotica in-game or on Discord.
  • No NSFW avatars, audios, decals, or outfits.

8. Punishments & Appeals

  • Violations may result in warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
  • Appeals must be submitted via Discord tickets and will be reviewed within 72 hours.
