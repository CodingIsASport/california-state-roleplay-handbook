1. Staff Authority & Purpose

Staff members are responsible for enforcing server rules, maintaining fair roleplay, and ensuring a professional and enjoyable environment for all players.

2. Staff Roles & Permissions

  • Director Level: Final authority over all server and staff decisions.
  • Executive Board: Oversees server operations, policy enforcement, and staff leadership.
  • Senior High Rank: Manages investigations, staff discipline, and administrator oversight.
  • High Rank: Performs active moderation and punishment enforcement.
  • Low Rank: Entry-level staff under supervision and training.

3. Staff Code of Conduct

  • Staff must act professionally at all times.
  • Abuse of power, favoritism, or bias is prohibited.
  • All staff actions must align with server policies.
  • Respect the Chain of Command at all times.

4. Communication & Activity

  • Staff must monitor in-game and Discord communications while on duty.
  • Tickets and reports must be handled promptly.
  • Activity requirements are set by management and enforced consistently.

5. Training & Documentation

  • Required training must be completed before active moderation.
  • Probationary staff must shadow senior staff.
  • All punishments and incidents must be properly logged.

6. Discipline & Strikes

  • Violations of staff policy may result in strikes, suspension, or removal.
  • Serious offenses may result in immediate termination.
  • Disciplinary actions are reviewed by management.

7. Professional Expectations

  • Lead by example in all interactions.
  • Remain calm and respectful in stressful situations.
  • Mentor and support lower-ranked staff.
