Staff Overview

Staff members enforce rules, manage RP, and maintain community standards. All staff must act professionally and follow the Chain of Command.

1. Staff Roles & Permissions

  • Director, Deputy Director, Assistant Director: Final authority, server decisions, staff approvals.
  • Ownership Board: Board of Directors, Chief of Staff, Staff Supervisor, Community Management — oversee server operations and staff performance.
  • Executive Board: Head Management, Senior Management, Management — supervise high-rank staff and review punishments.
  • Senior High Rank: Chief/Deputy/Internal Affairs, Head & Senior Administrators — investigate staff misconduct, supervise admins.
  • High Rank: Administrators, Junior Administrators, Head/Senior/Moderators — active moderation, punishments, ticket handling.
  • Low Rank: Entry-level support — shadowing, learning, and assisting higher ranks.

2. Staff Code of Conduct

  • Remain professional and unbiased.
  • No favoritism or personal targeting.
  • Respect Chain of Command.
  • Communicate respectfully in chat and VC.
  • Document all major actions (warnings, kicks, bans).

3. Communication & Activity

  • Monitor in-game chat and Discord when moderating.
  • Join VC when requested for moderation or events.
  • Respond to staff tickets and pings promptly.
  • Maintain minimum activity standards as required by management.

4. Training & Documentation

  • Complete required staff training before active moderation.
  • Shadow senior staff during probationary period.
  • Log punishments and incidents accurately.
  • Provide proof (screenshots, video clips) when necessary.

5. Discipline & Strikes

  • Violating staff rules may result in warnings, strikes, suspension, demotion, or removal.
  • Serious offenses (abuse of power, hate speech, leaking info) may result in immediate removal.
  • Strike system example: 1st strike = warning, 2nd = suspension, 3rd = removal.

6. Professional Expectations

  • Lead by example in all interactions.
  • Stay calm during stressful situations.
  • Always ask questions privately if unsure.
  • Respect higher ranks and mentor lower ranks.
