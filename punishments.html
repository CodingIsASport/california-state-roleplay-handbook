Punishment Philosophy

Punishments in California State Roleplay are designed to be fair, progressive, and based on evidence. Staff should always aim to correct behavior—not punish out of anger.

Progressive Discipline Ladder

Punishments should increase based on repetition and severity:

  1. Verbal Warning
  2. Written Warning / Staff Note
  3. Kick
  4. Temporary Ban
  5. Permanent Ban
  6. Blacklist (If Applicable)

Minor Offenses

These should start with warnings and escalate if repeated:

  • Fail Roleplay
  • Minor disrespect toward players
  • Traffic violations
  • Low-quality RP

Moderate Offenses

These typically result in warnings or kicks first:

  • RDM / VDM
  • Staff disrespect
  • Repeated Fail RP
  • Trolling / provoking situations
  • Ignoring staff instructions

Major Offenses

These usually require immediate temporary bans or higher:

  • Exploiting or hacking
  • Targeted harassment or hate speech
  • Ban evasion
  • Threats to players or staff
  • Mass trolling or server disruption

Example Punishment Guide

This chart is a basic enforcement reference:

  • Fail RP: Warn → Kick → 12-24 hr Ban
  • RDM/VDM: Kick → 12-48 hr Ban → 3-7 Day Ban
  • Staff Disrespect: Warn → Kick → 24 hr Ban
  • Exploiting: 7+ Day Ban → Permanent Ban → Blacklist

Evidence Requirements

All moderate and major punishments should include proof:

  • Screenshots for chat harassment
  • Clips for combat violations
  • Logs for bans
  • Staff documentation for repeat offenders

Appeal Rights

Players may appeal bans, but appeals must be:

  • Respectful
  • Honest with details
  • Submitted through the proper platform
  • Reviewed by senior staff only

Lying or hostility during appeals may result in denial.
