Purpose of Moderation

The role of staff moderation is to maintain high-quality roleplay, prevent harassment, enforce rules, and ensure all players have a fair and enjoyable experience.

When to Step In

  • Rule violations are observed (RDM, VDM, Fail RP, harassment).
  • RP is disrupted or chaotic.
  • A player requests staff intervention.
  • Evidence of exploitation or cheating appears.

How to Handle Situations

  1. Stay calm and neutral. Avoid bias.
  2. Gather all necessary information.
  3. Determine which rules were broken.
  4. Apply the minimum necessary punishment.
  5. Record the incident if required by staff policy.

Conflict Resolution Model

  • Listen: Hear all sides of the situation.
  • Gather Info: Collect evidence like screenshots, clips, or logs.
  • Review: Check against the rulebook.
  • Decide: Apply fair consequences.
  • Record: Document for transparency.

Scene Control

Staff are allowed to manage RP scenes to prevent chaos or rule-breaking:

  • Freeze the scene if needed
  • Move participants to safety
  • Request officers to clear a perimeter
  • Pause RP during investigations if necessary

Staff vs Player Guidelines

  • Never insult or argue with players.
  • Always explain rules calmly.
  • Escalate conflicts to senior staff if resolution fails.
  • Avoid using staff powers for personal benefit.

Examples of Staff Abuse

  • Kicking or banning players due to personal bias.
  • Teleporting or respawning players unnecessarily.
  • Harassing or mocking users.
  • Ignoring rule violations by friends or favored players.
