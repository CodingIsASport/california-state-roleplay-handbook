Moderation Policy

This page outlines how staff members are expected to moderate and enforce rules within CSRP.

1.0 Staff Authority

  • 1.1 Staff are authorized to enforce all server rules.
  • 1.2 Staff actions must be unbiased and professional.
  • 1.3 Abuse of staff powers is strictly prohibited.

2.0 Professional Conduct

  • 2.1 Staff must remain calm and respectful at all times.
  • 2.2 Staff may not argue with players publicly.
  • 2.3 Staff must not use sarcasm, insults, or threats.

3.0 Moderation Procedures

  • 3.1 Staff should investigate before taking action.
  • 3.2 Evidence should be collected when possible.
  • 3.3 Verbal warnings should be used when appropriate.
  • 3.4 Punishments must match severity.

4.0 In-Game Interventions

  • 4.1 Scene freezes should only be used when necessary.
  • 4.2 Staff must clearly explain why a scene is stopped.
  • 4.3 RP should resume as soon as possible.

5.0 Reports & Tickets

  • 5.1 All player reports must be handled seriously.
  • 5.2 False reports are punishable.
  • 5.3 Internal Affairs handles staff reports.

6.0 Confidentiality

  • 6.1 Staff information must remain private.
  • 6.2 Leaking staff discussions is prohibited.
